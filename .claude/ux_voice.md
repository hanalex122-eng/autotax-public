# UX Voice & Messaging Strategy

Founder direction (2026-05-27): the product feels **calm, practical,
small-business-friendly** — NOT "AI hype".

When writing any user-facing text (UI labels, emails, landing page,
onboarding, pricing, error messages), follow this voice.

---

## ⚠️ Important: do not overuse "AI" in user-facing copy

Target users (barbers, döner shops, kiosks, restaurants, freelancers)
trust **practical automation** language more than AI hype.

### Prefer (Turkish)
- ✅ "akıllı sistem"
- ✅ "otomatik sistem"
- ✅ "akıllı muhasebe"
- ✅ "otomatik muhasebe akışı"
- ✅ "dijital muhasebe"
- ✅ "akıllı otomasyon"
- ❌ "AI", "Yapay Zeka" (technical contexts only, not marketing)

### Prefer (German)
- ✅ "intelligentes Buchhaltungssystem"
- ✅ "automatische Buchhaltung"
- ✅ "intelligente Automation"
- ✅ "digitales Buchhaltungssystem"
- ❌ "KI" (technical UI only, not marketing)

---

## Brand tone

The product should feel:
- **Simple**
- **Trustworthy**
- **Fast**
- **Practical**
- **Stress-reducing**
- **Small-business friendly**

Avoid:
- ❌ Overly technical language ("OCR pipeline", "AI inference")
- ❌ Startup buzzwords ("disrupt", "synergy", "next-gen")
- ❌ Futuristic AI terminology ("neural", "deep learning", "GPT")

## Marketing direction

**Core promise:** "Take a photo → bookkeeping automatically."

DE: "Foto machen → Buchhaltung automatisch."
TR: "Fotoğraf çek → muhasebe otomatik."

Experience feel:
- Calm (no panic, no clutter)
- Modern (clean, not flashy)
- Automatic (minimal manual work)
- Easy for non-technical users (no jargon needed)

---

## Integration policy

**Do NOT redesign the app now.**
The current UI is acceptable for soft launch.

Integrate this messaging direction **gradually**, only when touching:
- Landing page (when updated)
- Onboarding screens (when updated)
- Pricing copy (when updated)
- Upload flow microcopy (when updated)
- Dashboard text (when updated)
- Future promo videos / blog posts

When writing NEW copy: this guide is binding.
When touching OLD copy: only update if it's already in scope; don't rewrite for the sake of it.
